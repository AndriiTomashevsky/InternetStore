InternetStore: online store of fans and accessories.
Presentation: https://goo.gl/Ub8Nbr
Internet store has Users with different roles and possibilities: employees, managers and administrators.
Customers can operate with products items in their current cart. Employees can operate with
Orders (Shipping), Managers can operate with Products and Categories (Add, Edit, Delete), and only
Administrator have permission to deal with Users and Roles (Add, Edit, Delete). There is a possibility
to search and sorting products of some parameters during the browsing.
